Frequently Asked Questions

Can i have covid-19 with-out a fever?

Yes

Primary symptoms of covid-19?

Head-ache, breathlessness, loss in smell and taste.

Can people with mid covid-19 sympotoms recover at home?

Yes, however it takes up to 14 days.

When should i seek emergency care if i have covid-19?

Trouble breathing, pain or pressure in the chest

How to protect yourself & others?

Get vaccinated, wear mask, avoid crowds.
